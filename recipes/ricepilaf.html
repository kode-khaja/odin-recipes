Rice Pilaf

Homemade Rice Pilaf:

Rice Pilaf has been around since probably the start of agriculture and since civilizations figured out how to make use of grains.

Every culture has a version of the rice and meat dish. Today, we will show you the most basic yet delicious rice pilaf here at Odin Recipes.

Ingredients

  • Long Grain Rice
  • Meat (Chicken/Lamb will do!)
  • Ghee
  • Garam Masala

Steps:

  1. Marinate Meat with Garam Masala
  2. Add Ghee to the pot and fry the marinated meat
  3. Pour water halfway to the pot and let it come to boil
  4. Add rice to the boiling meat water and close the lid
  5. Cook for 15 minutes on medium heat
  6. ENJOY!
