Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a staple in breakfast foods. They are easy to make but also easy to mess up.

Follow along to Odin's easy recipe to make yourself a plate of this fluffy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Sour cream - 1tbsp (thickener) OR
  • Whole Milk - 3-4tbsp (thickener)
  • Butter - 1tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix the eggs with thickener
  2. Fry Butter till it melts
  3. Pour mixed eggs and lower heat
  4. Keep the eggs moving as they are being slow cooked
  5. Turn off the stove when the eggs reach a creamy/semi solid consistency
  6. Add salt/pepper till it satisfies your needs
  7. ENJOY!
